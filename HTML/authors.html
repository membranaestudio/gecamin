Requirements to submit an abstract

Submit a 300 to 600-word abstract of your work, with a maxium of two figures (tables, graphic or image) in Word format and in English or Spanish, by April 15, 2024, using the file transfer site available for this purpose. Click on the "Submit Abstract HERE" button, located on the top side of the page.

Abstracts should describe the objective of the work and a synthesis of the obtained results, as well as include full contact details of the authors. The “Abstract Template” button above contains the Word template with predetermined formatting to prepare your abstract. Also, the format includes editorial guidelines to be followed for the abstract to be approved.

Don't miss the opportunity to share experiences and studies Water Congress 2024 as a presenting author.

Notes

Due Technical Program restrictions, an author is allow to present a maximum of 2 works in oral presentation (additional works can be submitted for Poster presentation). A company is allow to present a maximum of 3 works in oral presentation (additional works can be submitted for Poster presentation).

Important note for mining company authors: Due to data confidentiality issues, mining authors may opt to submit only an abstract, written in English, of 300 to 600 words, without having to submit the full article.

Review
Process

All submissions received by Water Congress 2024 are subject to all stages of the review process in order to be approved for publication in the proceedings and included in the technical program.

Stage 1: Article Selection

All abstracts will be reviewed and commented on by Water Congress 2024’s Editorial Committee. Approval of an abstract at this stage does not automatically guarantee that the final version will be accepted and included in the program. The authors of accepted abstracts will receive comments to consider and include in the final version of their abstract

Stage 2: Final Article Approval

Final abstracts will be reviewed by the Editorial Committee to ensure that all modifications have been made. At this stage, abstracts will be confirmed for oral or poster presentation. Final abstracts approved for publication will be published in the Water Congress 2024 proceedings. At this stage authors must register to Water Congress 2024 to confirm their participation.

Stage 3: Presentation Selection

All authors of final articles approved for oral presentation will be asked to send the PowerPoint presentation that they will exhibit in their 20-minute talk.
