With the objective of collaborating with the promotion of research carried out by last year's grad students, and strengthening the professional development of them, Gecamin has decided to finance scholarships to be used by students whose area of Research is related to the conference and may be presented in a Poster format during Water Congress 2024.

Requirements to access the scholarship

The scholarship covers full registration to attend Water Congress 2024, with access to all the technical and social activities.

If you are interested in obtaining one of the scholarships available, write an email and find out more about the requirements on how to apply watercongress@gecamin.com

The requirements to access the scholarship are to:

1.

Be recommended by an authority of the department of the university where you study.

2.

Submit an abstract of your Poster until July 29, 200-300 words, in English or Spanish and in Word format. In the “Abstract Template” button you will find the document.

3.

If your abstract is accepted, prepare and submit your work in a poster format. Above you will find the Poster Preparation Guide and the Poster Demo.
