Cancelation policy

Cancellations will be accepted only if done in written form before July 4, 2024 to watercongress@gecamin.com. The cancelation must include the name of the participant, company and reason for cancelation. After the conference is over, the registration fee will be reimbursed minus the bank transfer costs and a charge of USD 300 / CLP 236,000 for authors or a charge of USD 150 / CLP 118,000 for other categories and a digital copy of the congress book will be sent to the participant.

After July 4, 2024 there will be no refunds and all registered participants will be charged the full fee. If a registered participant is unable to attend and is not able to cancel before July 4, 2024, replacements are welcome at no additional cost. Please notify us of any substitutions (providing full name, ID, position, company, phone number and email to watercongress@gecamin.com.)

Photo and video release

By accepting the terms and conditions of registration, you allow Gecamin Ltda. to take photographs or videos of you during the congress for use on their website and in future promotional materials. If you do not wish to be included in any photo or video, please write oscar.farias@gecamin.com at least 72 hours prior to the event.

Contact information release

By accepting the terms and conditions of registration, you allow Gecamin Ltda. to share your contact information with fellow congress participants after the congress has ended. If you do NOT want your contact details released, please email comercial@gecamin.com before the beginning of the congress.
