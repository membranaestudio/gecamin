Water Congress 2024 is an opportunity to learn and exchange knowledge, practices and solutions over the most relevant water management issues related to the global industry. The congress brings together executives, professionals and academics mainly from areas related to the environment, sustainability and water management.

Committees

The Organizing, Advisory and Editorial committees support the promotion of the conference. Likewise, they support by determining the technical focus of the conference program. The editorial committee reviews the written works that are received, establishing the highest technical quality. All committee members have a special discount on registrations.
